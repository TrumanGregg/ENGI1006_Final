Spring 2020 Courses:

Course Title Instructor Course Description
COMS W1004 Adam Cannon An introduction to computer science for computer science to teach students fundamental Java programming skills, knowledge of the fundamental concepts in computer science, and algorithmic problem-solving capabilities.
ELEN E1201 David Vallancourt An introduction to electrical engineering covering simple circuits, op amps, RC circuits, RL circuits, transformers, AC, and diodes.
ENGI E1006 Timothy Paine A practical introduction to the field of computer science covering the fundamentals of data structures, algorithms, and the python language.
ENGL CC1010 Rachel Rueckert A course to teach undergraduates to read and write essays in order to participate in intellectual communities at Columbia and beyond.
PHYS UN1402 Georgia Karagiorgi An introduction to the concepts of electricity, magnetism, and optics with basic working knowledge of Calculus assumed.
PHYS UN1405 Georgia Karagiorgi Recitation for PHYS UN1402 to help students better understand the material and ask questions about concepts or problems they don't understand.

Favorite Class:

Personally, my favorite class this semester has been COMS W1004. Over the course of this semester, I have learning a lot about programming and enjoy the creative projects due every other week. One such project was programming video poker in Java and then playing it with my family. Although challenging, I thought that the material was reasonable and stimulating.
